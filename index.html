Food Fight

You are alone in this war zone. Everyone is enemy. Pancakes and eggs flying around trying to hit you. Obliterate your enemies with your mighty pancakes. You will move following the cursor and click the left button to throw your pancake. Remember you must not get too dirty or your mom will be angry, you can only take at most 5 hits. Survive and win this Food Fight.

Arts by: Peony

Music by: ZomBCool

Play on itch.io

Gid's Experience

Gid's experience is a free game where you play as a tank named Gid. This is just a prototype and the game is over once the boss is defeated. Press left and right arrow key to rotate the tank, and moving forward and backward by using up and down arrow key respectively. Press space bar to shoot projectiles. Enjoy!
